---
title: "Enabling Developer Mode"
description: "You are required to enable developer mode to sideload."
---

<Warning>
  Enabling developer mode will **reduce security**, as noted in [Apple's Documentation](https://developer.apple.com/documentation/xcode/enabling-developer-mode-on-a-device). If you are concerned over security risks, you can read [Apple's Documentation](https://developer.apple.com/documentation/xcode/enabling-developer-mode-on-a-device).
</Warning>

Open the **Settings App** and **scroll down** until you see **Privacy & Security**. Then click Privacy & Security.

![Privacy and Security](/images/others/privacy_and_security.jpeg)

Now **scroll to the bottom** of **Privacy & Security**, where you'll see the Developer Mode option. Enabling this will ask you to restart your device if your on iOS 18 or higher. Click restart and wait for your iDevice to turn off. Then hold the power button to turn on your iDevice. When its on, swipe up and click enable Developer Mode.

![Developer Mode](/images/others/developer_mode.jpeg)
